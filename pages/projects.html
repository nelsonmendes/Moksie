SOUNDS

Moksie Sounds is a simple stand-alone tool for editing and manipulating sounds. Assumed to have a rather complete sonoteca (with music and effects) included in the application. This application will allow users who have little to no experience in music and / or sound design to quickly select and process audio clips. This application therefore allows the recording and processing of sounds by use of a predefined set of effects, which can then be incorporated in video games or other projects.
